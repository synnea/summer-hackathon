What is IconMan?

IconMan is an event that covers a full weekend during summer and combines both physical and intelectual challenges.

A team competition to push the limits of your abilities of coding, building machines and show physical strength.

Alternatively we present IconKids where we have a highly trained team of tutors and child minders to present your children notions of coding and robotics in a funny and interactive way.

All our activities are catered to bring the whole experience outside and enjoy a full weekend of fun, games, learning and physical activities!

IconMan

Behold the ultimate challenge. Something no one has ever seen before!

The IconMan Arena where the competitors will face the challenge to build a machine and compete! Our competitors will face different physical challenges to have the chance to win unique parts or expert help to boost their project!

Every event will present a different theme and the participants will have to build their machines to face those specific challenges! From rocket launches to hovercrafts, from trebuchets to robotic hands...this is the event where the mental and physical attributes meet to crown the IconMan!

IconKids

The perfect place to bring your children this summer! Either if you are participating on the competitions or to give the little ones a chance to learn important skills while having fun and let off some steam with great outdoors activities.

We have a great team of professionals especially trained to teach children and they will be leading the activities, guiding your little ones to knoledge and loads of fun!

The IconKids event offers the opportunity for children to join team competitions and join courses on coding, robotics and many other.
