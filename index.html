What is IconMan?

IconMan is an event that covers a full weekend during summer and combines both physical and intellectual challenges.

It is a team competition to push the limits of your abilities to code, build machines and show physical strength.

Teams of four will sign up to compete in the making of a physical product prototype. But while most hackathons focus only on hacking ability, we also include aethletic competition. Compete in events such as running, basketball, and swimming to get access to robotic parts and tools!

Alternatively we present IconKids where we have a highly trained team of tutors to present your children notions of coding and robotics in a fun and interactive way.

All our activities are catered to bring the whole experience outside and enjoy a full weekend of fun, games, learning and physical activities!

Our Events

The ultimate challenge awaits...

The IconMan Arena is where the competitors will face the challenge to build a machine and compete! Our competitors will face different physical challenges to have the chance to win unique parts or expert help to boost their project!

Being the best hacker is not enough. Just as a triathlete cannot just excel at one of the three disciplines that make up a triathlon, to success at IconMan, teams must excel physically as well as intellectually.

Ever dream of creating your own product? A robot perhaps, a drone, a trebuchet or a frisbee with a twist...come home with a unique product made by yourself and one of our prizes valued up to €5000.

The perfect place to bring your children this summer, either to participate in various coding-related courses and competitions or to let off some steam with great outdoors activities.

We have a great team of professionals specially trained to teach children and they will be leading the activities, guiding your little ones to knoledge and loads of fun!

The IconKids event offers the opportunity for children to join team competitions and join courses on coding, robotics and many other.

IconMan isn't just about hacking and intellectual prowress, though. Compete in physical activities to boost your product!

Basketball, baseball, sprinting... a variety of activities await in a total of 5 physical challenges each team must overcome.

You don't have to sweat for free, though. Do you want advice from an experienced professional in the field of your product? Maybe get a certain tool you need to perfect your vision?

Well, looks like there's no way around getting a little sweaty.
